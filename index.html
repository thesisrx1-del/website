Frequently Asked

Questions Residents Ask

Is this legal and ethical? +
We provide academic writing assistance and research support — similar to the support provided by statisticians, scientific editors, or professional medical writers who routinely assist in thesis and publication writing in India and globally. You remain the primary researcher and the intellectual owner of your work.
Will my guide/university find out? +
No. We operate under complete confidentiality. We do not maintain any public records of clients. Every engagement is discreet. Our service relationship stays between you and us.
How do I know the work will be good enough for my guide? +
We offer revision rounds as part of every package. If your guide asks for changes, we'll incorporate them. We've worked with strict guides across institutions — managing feedback and revisions is part of what we do.
How do I pay? Is it safe? +
We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major payment apps. Our payment structure is straightforward: a small advance is collected to begin work, and the remaining balance is collected in full before the final draft is released to you.

This entire arrangement runs on mutual trust — we trust you to honour the commitment you make when you reach out to us, and you trust us to deliver work that is worth every rupee. We put in significant time, research, and expertise into every project. Releasing final work only after complete payment ensures that our effort is respected, just as we respect your deadline and your trust in us. We have never failed a client who kept their word — and we do not intend to start.
What if my IEC rejects the protocol? +
We'll revise the protocol based on the IEC's specific objections at no additional charge (for one round of IEC-related revisions). Our protocols are written with common IEC concerns in mind.
My thesis submission is in 3 months. Is that enough time? +
Depends on the service. Topic + Protocol can be done in 1–2 weeks. Full thesis writing needs 4–10 weeks depending on data availability and word count. Fill the form and share your timeline — we'll tell you honestly whether we can help.
Do I need to collect data or will you do that too? +
You collect the clinical data from your hospital — that's your role as the researcher. We handle designing data collection tools, analysis, writing, and interpretation. If you've already collected data, even better.
